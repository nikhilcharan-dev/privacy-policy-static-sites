Privacy Policy for OwlCoders Browser Extension

Last updated: [15-11-2025]

1. Introduction

OwlCoders Browser Extension (“the Extension”) helps users monitor AI website usage with full consent. We respect your privacy and protect your information responsibly.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following information only when monitoring is enabled:

  • AI website domains you visit
  • Timestamps of visits
  • Your login credentials

3. How We Use the Information

Your data is used to:

  • Display your AI usage logs inside the extension
  • (Optional) Send logs to your institution’s backend server

We do not sell or share your information with any third-party services.

4. Data Storage

Data is stored either locally on your device or (optional) in your institution’s server.

5. Permissions

The Extension uses browser permissions like tabs and storage. We do not read chat content or personal data.

6. User Consent

Tracking is disabled by default. You must explicitly enable it.

7. Security

We use reasonable security measures, but no system is perfect.
